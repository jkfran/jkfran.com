How it works

This tool uses RMBG-1.4, a neural network by BRIA AI trained to detect and remove backgrounds from images. The model runs entirely in your browser using Transformers.js and WebAssembly — no images are uploaded to any server.

Tips for best results

  • Clear subjects — People, products, animals with distinct edges
  • Good lighting — Contrast between subject and background
  • Simple backgrounds — Solid or blurred backgrounds work best
  • First use — Model (~40 MB) downloads once, then cached
  • Processing time — Usually 2-10 seconds depending on device
  • File size — Images up to 10 MB supported
