How it works

This tool queries DNS NS (nameserver) records via Cloudflare's DNS-over-HTTPS API directly from your browser. Every registered domain has nameserver records. If a domain returns no NS records (NXDOMAIN), it is very likely available for registration.

Privacy

Unlike most domain checkers, this tool sends zero data to our servers. The DNS query goes directly from your browser to Cloudflare's public resolver (1.1.1.1) — the same resolver your browser may already use. No domain names you check are logged, tracked, or visible to us. Your ideas stay yours.

Accuracy

DNS-based checks are approximately 95% accurate. A small number of domains may show as available when they are actually reserved, premium-priced, or in a redemption grace period. Always confirm availability at your preferred registrar before purchasing.
